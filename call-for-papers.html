Call for Papers

The Department of Linguistics at the University of California, Santa Cruz is pleased to announce that the 35th annual meeting of Formal Approaches to Slavic Linguistics (FASL 35) will be held May 1st–3rd, 2026 at our campus in Santa Cruz, CA, USA.

Invited Speakers:

  • Miloje Despic (Cornell University)
  • Vera Gribanova (Stanford University)
  • Gaja Jarosz (University of Massachusetts Amherst)

Abstracts are invited for 20-minute talks and posters on all topics in formal Slavic linguistics, including phonetics, phonology, morphology, syntax, semantics, and pragmatics. We welcome theoretical work in any of these domains, and experimental work that speaks to linguistic theory is additionally welcome.
