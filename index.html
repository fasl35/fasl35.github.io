The 35th iteration of Formal Approaches to Slavic Linguistics (FASL 35) will be hosted by the Linguistics Department at the University of California, Santa Cruz on May 1st–3rd, 2026. The conference will take place in-person at UC Santa Cruz. In addition, a select number of sessions will be offered fully online to accommodate participants who may not be able to attend otherwise.

For questions, please contact the organizers at fasl35@ucsc.edu.
